8. Santuario di San Bernardino alle Ossa

📍 Piazza Santo Stefano, Milan

🕍 A unique Catholic sanctuary famous for its ossuary – a small chapel decorated with human skulls and bones, built in the 13th century.

⏰ Opening Hours:
– Monday to Friday: 8:00 – 18:00
– Saturday: 9:30 – 18:00
– Sunday: 9:30 – 12:00
The ossuary is closed on Sundays but otherwise follows the same hours.

🆓 Free entry. Donations are welcome. Please note this is a place of worship, not a museum – respectful behavior is required during visits.

About Brera

Brera is Milan’s bohemian and artistic neighborhood, filled with charm, cobbled streets, art galleries, boutique shops and cozy cafés. It’s a perfect area for a cultural walk and a relaxed aperitivo. The heart of Brera is bordered by Via Pontaccio, Via Fatebenefratelli, Via Borgonuovo, Via Monte di Pietà, Via dell’Orso, Via Ponte Vetero, and Via Mercato.

1. Pinacoteca di Brera

📍 Via Brera 28, Milan

🖼️ One of Italy’s most important art galleries, home to masterpieces by Caravaggio, Raphael, Mantegna, Tintoretto, and the iconic painting The Kiss by Hayez.

⏰ Opening Hours: Tuesday – Sunday, 8:30am – 7:15pm (last entrance at 6pm)
❌ Closed: Mondays and December 25th
🆓 Free admission (with reservation) on the first Sunday of each month

2. Brera Botanical Garden

📍 Via Fiori Oscuri 4, Milan (Entrances also from Via Brera 28 and Via Privata Fratelli Gabba 10)

🌿 A peaceful green oasis in the heart of Brera, offering a quiet retreat among medicinal plants, ancient trees and floral displays.

🚇 Easily reachable on foot from the metro stops Montenapoleone, Lanza or Cairoli.

ℹ️ More info: Visit the Garden – Official Site

3. Chiesa di Santa Maria del Carmine

📍 Piazza del Carmine 2, Milan

⛪ A 15th-century Romanesque church with Baroque interior, featuring impressive frescoes, paintings and statues.

⏰ Opening Hours: Usually open daily, from early morning to early evening (exact hours may vary slightly depending on services).
📅 Visitors wishing to enter the church interior should check mass times: View agenda
