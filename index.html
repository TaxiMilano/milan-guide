Discover Milan by Neighborhood

1. Duomo di Milano

⏰ Open daily: Cathedral and Archaeological Area 9:00–18:00; Terraces until 19:00 (Thu) or 20:00 (Fri–Sun).

🎟️ Tickets: Cathedral €5; Terraces (stairs) €10; with lift €15; Combo (Duomo + terraces + museum) from €20. Official site

📍 Piazza del Duomo, Milan

2. Museo del Novecento

🖼️ 20th-century Italian art with a stunning view of the Duomo from the top floor.

🎟️ €5 (full), €3 (reduced) – Buy tickets

📍 Piazza del Duomo 8, Milan

3. Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II

🛍️ Elegant 19th-century shopping arcade. Famous for its bull mosaic “good luck spin”.

🆓 Free entry

📍 Piazza del Duomo, Milan
