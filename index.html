Milan Tourist Guide

⭐1. Piazza Duomo & Milan Cathedral

Start your journey at Piazza del Duomo, the heart of Milan. Visit the magnificent Duomo di Milano, a Gothic masterpiece and one of the world’s largest cathedrals.

Don't miss: Rooftop terraces for panoramic city views.

🎟️ Tickets: Buy online or on-site (long lines possible).

📍 Location: Piazza del Duomo
🕒 Recommended visit time: 1–2 hours

🖼️2. Museo del Novecento

Just steps from the Duomo, explore this modern art museum featuring Italian and international works from the 20th century.

Highlights: Works by Boccioni, Fontana, Kandinsky, and more. Rooftop view of the Duomo.

🎟️ Tickets: €5 (full), €3 (reduced). Buy here.

📍 Location: Via Marconi 1
🕒 Recommended visit time: 1–1.5 hours

🏛️3. Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II

One of the oldest shopping malls in the world, known as "il salotto buono" — the elegant living room of Milan.

Highlights: Iron-and-glass architecture, luxury shops, historic cafés.

🍀 Fun Fact: Spin three times on the bull mosaic for good luck!

🎟️ Entry: Free
📍 Location: Between Duomo and La Scala
🕒 Recommended visit time: 30–45 minutes

🎭4. Piazza della Scala & Teatro alla Scala

Cross the Galleria to reach Piazza della Scala and visit the legendary opera house built in 1778.

🎟️ Visit: Museum & Theater tours
🎫 Shows: Book tickets
👗 Dress Code: Elegant attire required

Also in the square: Leonardo da Vinci statue, Palazzo Marino (City Hall)

📍 Location: Piazza della Scala
🕒 Recommended time: 45 min – 1.5 hours (more for shows)

🥐5. A Delicious Break at Luini

Try Luini’s famous panzerotti — stuffed baked pastries, sweet or savory, loved by locals since 1949.

🍴 Must-try: Classic tomato and mozzarella panzerotto
📄 Menu

⚠️ Allergies: Ask for the allergen list.

📍 Location: Via Santa Radegonda 16
🕒 Stop time: 15–30 minutes (lines at lunch)

🛍️6. Shopping at La Rinascente

Luxury department store right next to the Duomo featuring top brands and a rooftop with food & drinks.

🛒 Brand List
🍹 Rooftop restaurants

📍 Location: Via Santa Radegonda 3
🕒 Time: 1–2 hours (or more for drinks)
