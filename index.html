About the Area

This district combines art, history, and green spaces. Easily reachable by metro M1 (Red Line), stop at Cairoli. For a scenic route, walk from Cordusio Square (with the Starbucks Reserve Roastery) down Via Dante until you reach Piazza Castello.

1. The Sforza Castle / Castello Sforzesco

📍 Piazza Castello
🏰 A Renaissance fortress housing multiple museums and Michelangelo's Pietà Rondanini.
🎟️ Two separate tickets available:

  • Museum ticket
  • Rooftop view ticket (purchase on site)
⏰ Museums: Mon–Sun 10:00–17:00 (last entry)
🆓 Courtyards: Daily 07:00–19:30 (free)
Google Maps

2. Parco Sempione / Simplon Park

📍 Google Maps
🌳 Fully pedestrian park accessible through the castle. Highlights include:

  • 🧚‍♀️ Ponte delle Sirenette
  • 🏟️ Parco Amphitheatre
  • 🎭 Teatro Continuo di Alberto Burri
☕ Bars in the park:
  • Bar Bianco
  • Chiosco Giò
🚰 Refill water at “vedovelle” fountains.
Other sites nearby: Triennale, Arena Civica, Torre Branca, Acquario Civico.
